About Our New Club

The Earl Marriott Computer Science Club was formed in the last 4 weeks of the school year. As a brand-new club, we're in our foundational phase—learning, building, and setting up for future success!

While we haven't competed in any events or earned awards yet, we're excited about what's to come and are working hard to prepare for next year's opportunities.

What We've Been Doing

In our short time together, we've focused on building our foundation:

  • Learning programming basics together (Python, JavaScript, and web development)
  • Setting up our club structure and meeting schedule
  • Creating this website as our first collaborative project
  • Exploring different areas of computer science to find our interests
  • Building teamwork and problem-solving skills

Our Goals for Next Year

Now that we've established ourselves, we're excited to achieve great things in the upcoming school year:

  • Participate in local and regional programming competitions
  • Develop and showcase our own software projects
  • Host coding workshops for fellow students
  • Collaborate with other school clubs on tech-related initiatives
  • Earn our first awards and recognitions as a club

Stay tuned—our best work is still ahead!
